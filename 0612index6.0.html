    How it works

    A simple 3-step process to rebuild your library locally in your browser.

    💻
    Active

    Connect your browser

    Save a helper bookmark to enable library extraction.

    GOG Helper
    ⚡ Ready to copy / Drag to bar
    🌐
    Idle

    Open GOG

    Go to your GOG account and activate the helper tool.

    Then click “GOG Helper” from your bookmarks bar.
    📦
    Idle

    Export your library

    Download your games as a local JSON file.

    Drop my_gog_library.json here or click to upload
    🔒 Processed entirely in your browser
